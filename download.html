Installing Flarum is Easy

  1. Download and unzip Flarum onto your webserver (requires PHP 5.5+ and MySQL 5.5+).
  2. Go to your forum's root in a web browser.
  3. Enter your details and click "Install Flarum".

Upgrading from Beta 1

  1. Back up your database.
  2. Overwrite all files and folders except for assets.
  3. Go to your forum's root in a web browser.

Having trouble?

Check out the Installation and Troubleshooting docs.

We're really excited to get Flarum into your hands! However, for the sake of both our sanities, let's make a few things clear.

  • This is beta software. It has bugs and will probably break. (Help us make it better!)
  • Upgrading from beta to subsequent versions might be a little tricky. We'll do our best to ensure it's as easy as possible.
  • The take-home message is that you should not use this in production. This beta is solely intended to garner feedback from users and developers.
